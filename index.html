About

Work shaped by restraint, proximity, and consequence.

I write and direct psychologically immersive narrative films that move between realism and interior space. My work is drawn toward liminal states—grief, memory, transformation—and the quiet question of how a person becomes capable of sin.

Based in New York. Available for collaborations, select documentary work, and project inquiries.
