Le Rouge Originel

Feature Film · In Pre-Production · Summer 2026

A woman embeds herself in the life of a criminal, and slowly becomes capable of sin.

observation becomes participation

LOVE LED US TO ONE DEATH
Anne does not infiltrate — she remains.
Observation becomes continuity.
Violence is not rupture —
it is what goes uninterrupted.

presence without intervention

Influenced by Chantal Akerman, Michael Haneke, and Krzysztof Kieślowski, the film resists psychological explanation. Stillness replaces escalation. Silence replaces cueing. The camera does not align, console, or condemn.
