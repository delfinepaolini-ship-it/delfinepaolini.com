REDS HEARTEN

We descend for love.

These are not words I know
they are only way of know
what words
for the sense of you asleep.

Conceived as a companion to Le Rouge Originel, Reds Hearten widens the descent — into rupture, ritual, and the architecture of the underworld.

If Le Rouge Originel asks how a person becomes capable of sin, Reds Hearten asks what survives after crossing through.
