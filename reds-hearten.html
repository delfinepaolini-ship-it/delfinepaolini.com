REDS HEARTEN

These are not words I know
they are only ways of knowing
what words
for the sense of you asleep.

Conceived as a companion to Le Rouge Originel, Reds Hearten widens the descent — into rupture, repetition, and the architecture of atonement.

If Le Rouge Originel asks how a person becomes capable of sin, Reds Hearten asks what it costs to survive that capability.
