A Wonderful Way with Dragons

Feature · Written & Directed by Delfine Paolini
Watch Trailer Press Request Press Materials

After a pandemic sweeps the world, six children remain abandoned on an enigmatic island. Their attempt to build order quickly erodes into something more elemental—where survival becomes secondary to power, allegiance, and the fragile construction of identity.
