Upstate

Feature Film · In Development

In the summer of 1995, two best friends arrive at camp carrying the same private rupture: each has lost a parent to suicide. Their friendship—once effortless—begins to bend under the weight of shared grief. When one starts to drift inward, the other holds on too tightly, not yet understanding that love can wound as deeply as it saves.
