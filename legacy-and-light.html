Legacy & Light

Presence, witness, and legacy at the threshold of life.

Legacy & Light is a women-founded practice devoted to presence at the end of life — offering guided life reflection, and thoughtful preservation of personal legacy.

Rooted in quiet presence and deep listening, this work supports dignity and meaning at life’s threshold. Sessions include guided storytelling conversations, and service dog comfort visits.

As a storyteller whose work explores mortality and moral interiority, I have long been drawn to the quiet spaces at the edge of life. Legacy & Light extends that work beyond the screen — into lived presence.

Services

  • Quiet witness sessions
  • Guided legacy storytelling
  • Audio or written memory preservation
  • Family respite presence
  • Service dog comfort visits
